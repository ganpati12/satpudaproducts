Sabudana Papad

In stock.

As the name suggests sabudana papad is made from sabudana which are tapioca pearls. These papads are also called as sago papads. Usually sabudana papad is made by cooking the tapioca pearls till it reaches a porridge like consistency, which is then spread in small to medium circles and then sun dried.
